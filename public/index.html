About Our Philosophy

Biophilic Design at Its Core

At Fosyfol Studio, we believe that the best interiors are those that don't just look beautiful but feel alive. Our approach integrates living ecosystems directly into architectural spaces, creating environments that support human wellness and ecological balance.

Founded in 2015 in Portland, Oregon, we've pioneered nature-integrated design across the United States, working with residential clients, corporate offices, healthcare facilities, and hospitality venues to create spaces that breathe, grow, and evolve.

Our American-based team includes interior designers, botanists, light therapy specialists, and sustainability experts who collaborate to create truly holistic environments that reflect the diverse ecosystems of North America.

Our Services

Our Specialized Services

Living Wall Biome Curation

Design and maintenance of vertical ecosystems tailored to your space's light, humidity, and aesthetic requirements. We select native and adaptive plant species that purify air, regulate humidity, and create visual harmony specific to North American climates.

Light Spectrum Optimization

Custom lighting solutions that mimic natural daylight patterns to support circadian rhythms, enhance mood, and promote wellness in indoor environments throughout the diverse American climate zones.

Biophilic Space Planning

Holistic interior design that incorporates natural materials, organic forms, and strategic placement of living elements to create spaces that feel connected to the American landscape and regional ecosystems.
